In today's world, people are facing various pressures, and some individuals even lack a space to release their tension. Therefore, the website is created for people to freely and anonymously express themselves, much like a "tree hollow", where people can vent their feelings and get comfort. Upon visiting the site, users will encounter posts distributed across the homepage like bubbles. Click on the captivating bubbles to participate in discussions or create your own bubble to express and share your thoughts.
